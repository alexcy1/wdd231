Site Plan for Erruki News

Site Name

Erruki News

Erruki News represents a comprehensive platform dedicated to aggregating news from trusted external sources, such as the New York Times API. The name reflects the website's commitment to providing accurate and timely news updates while fostering a user-friendly environment. By using "Erruki," the site creates a unique identity that encourages users to engage with diverse news topics. The website, erruki-news.com, serves as a reliable resource for users seeking to stay informed on various issues while also offering features that enhance their reading experience. erruki-news.com

Site Purpose

Erruki News aims to aggregate top news stories from external sources like the New York Times API, providing users with a comprehensive and easily navigable platform to access diverse news categories. The site also offers services such as user profile creation, favorite news article saving. It focuses on keeping users informed by covering a wide range of news topics, including world events, technology, business, and style. The website is designed to be user-centric, offering features like a favorites page for signed-up users, an "About Us" page, and detailed information about its news services.
