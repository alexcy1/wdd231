Non Profit Membership Details

This membership is designed specifically for non-profit organizations within the Lagos business community. It provides benefits that address the unique needs of non-profits, including discounted membership rates, access to networking opportunities tailored to the non-profit sector, and specialized resources to support their causes and operations.

Cost

$0.00 No cost

Bronze Membership Details

The Bronze Membership serves as an entry-level plan for individuals or small businesses looking to get involved with the Lagos Chamber of Commerce. It offers basic access to resources, events, and services at an affordable rate, making it an ideal choice for startups or those new to the chamber wanting to explore its benefits before committing to higher tiers.

Cost

$200 per year

×

Silver Membership Details

The Silver Membership is a mid-tier option offering more extensive benefits than the Bronze plan. Members enjoy enhanced access to events, more substantial networking opportunities, and a broader range of resources aimed at helping businesses and individuals grow. This tier strikes a balance between cost and benefits, providing great value for those looking to expand their involvement in the chamber.

Cost

$350 per year

×

Gold Membership Details

The Gold Membership represents the premium option, offering maximum benefits and exclusive access to chamber resources. Gold members receive invitations to VIP events, special content, and personalized support to fully leverage their membership. This plan is ideal for businesses and individuals seeking top-tier engagement and opportunities within the Lagos Chamber of Commerce.

Cost

$500 per year
